Introduction

Wildlife refers to all animals, birds, insects, and organisms that live freely in nature. These animals play a very important role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem. Without wildlife, the environment would become unstable and life on Earth would be affected.

Conservation means protecting animals and their natural habitats so they do not become extinct. Human activities such as deforestation, hunting, and pollution are major threats to wildlife.
